Combinatorics

Name Formula
Combinations - unordered $$^{n}C_{r} = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!r!}$$
Permutations - ordered $$^{n}P_{r} = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$$
Circles For n seats around a circular table, the number of circular permutations is given by $$ (n-1)! $$
Pigeonhole Principle For n+1 pigeons and n pigeons, at least one pigeonhole will have at least 2 pigeons

Binomials

Name Formula
Friendly general form $$(x+y)^n = {}^{n}C_{0} \cdot x^{n}y^{0} + {}^{n}C_{1} \cdot x^{n-1}y^{1} + {}^{n}C_{2} \cdot x^{n-2}y^{2} ... {}^{n}C_{n-1} \cdot x^{1}y^{n-1} + {}^{n}C_{n} \cdot x^{0}y^{n}$$
Sigma notation $$\sum_{r=0}^{n} {}^{n}C_{r}x^{n-r}y^{r}$$
Pascal's triangle

Derivatives and calculus

Name Formula
Chain Rule $$\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \times \frac{du}{dx}$$
Product Rule $$\frac{dy}{dx} = vu' + uv'$$
Quotient rule $$\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac {vu' - uv'}{v^2}$$

Geometry

Name Formula
Volume of a sphere $$\frac{4}{3}\pi r^3$$
Surface area of a sphere $$4 \pi r^2$$
Volume of a cone $$\frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h$$
Surface area of a cone $$\pi r^2 + \pi rh$$
